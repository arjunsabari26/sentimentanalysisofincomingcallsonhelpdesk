About the Platform

AI Helpdesk Call Sentiment Analyzer is a web-based platform that analyzes incoming helpdesk call recordings and detects customer sentiment using Natural Language Processing (NLP). The system converts call audio into text and classifies the sentiment as positive, neutral, or negative.

It helps organizations monitor customer satisfaction, identify frustrated customers quickly, and improve support quality through real-time insights, dashboards, and analytics.
