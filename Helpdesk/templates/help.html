Help & Support

Welcome to the AI Helpdesk Call Sentiment Analyzer. This platform helps analyze customer call recordings and detect their sentiment automatically.

How to use the system:

  1. Login – Sign in using your registered email and password.
  2. Upload Call Recording – Go to the Upload section and upload a call audio file (.mp3 or .wav).
  3. Automatic Analysis – The system converts the audio into text and analyzes the sentiment using AI.
  4. View Results – Check the detected sentiment (Positive, Neutral, or Negative) along with the call transcript.
  5. Dashboard & Analytics – Use the dashboard to view statistics, trends, and overall customer satisfaction.
  6. Call History – Access previously analyzed calls and review their results anytime.
If you experience any issues while using the system, please contact the system administrator.
